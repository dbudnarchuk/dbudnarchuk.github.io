About our restaurant

Welcome to the home page for Daemon's restaurant, we are a family restaurant located in Edmonton, Alberta. Our goal is to make high quality dishes, at a price that is ridiculously high even for a restaurant! Founded in 2021, Daemon's restaurant has accumulated multiple local awards for being a fast growing and ambitious establishment. Due to the creative mind of the owner, and his talented team of staff, Daemon's restaurant has been wildly succesful in the Edmonton area, with people even coming from cities like Vancouver or Calgary to try the food.
